Welcome...

Located at the tip of the thumb is Port Austin, Michigan. This small town is right on Lake Huron boasting both sunrise and sunset views along the breakwall and countless other sights to take in during your trip. From kayaking to mini golf, Port Austin has no shortage of outdoor activities for your family or group. Stop by the farmers market in the morning, the farm to table restaurants in the evening, and finish the day at Port Austin’s best ice cream shop. No matter who you bring along for your travels, Port Austin has something for them.
